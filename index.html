Cheap

$4

  • 6-9 months in French or American Oak
  • Released after 1.5 years of aging
  • From large vineyard region
  • A developing wine nation
  • Affordable packaging
  • Small production is not possible
  • Machine harvested grapes
  • A mixed of wine grapes
  • Often contains residual sugar to add richness

VS

Expensive

$900

  • 12 months in new Bertranges French Oak Barrels
  • Released after 6 years of aging
  • From a specific vineyard region
  • A well-developed wine nation
  • Expensive packaging
  • Usually in small production
  • Hand harvested grapes
  • Premium single-varietal grapes
  • Little to no residual sugar
