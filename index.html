History of Wine


Although no exact date were known, archeologists have found traces of wine as early as 6000BC at Georgia, 5000BC in Iran and 7000BC in China. The earliest winery however were only known as early as 6100BC in Armenia called Areni-1 winery. Wine played an important role in ancient history both in culutral such as Greece and Rome as well as Religion such as Judaism and Kiddush.

How It's Made




It is produced through the process of fermentation of grapes. Yeast is added into the mixture and it consumes sugar in the grapes and convert it to ethanol,co2 and heat. Different mixture of grapes and yeast creates different type of wine. That's what makes wine special because there is no right or wrong way to make one.

Type of Grape (Variety)


Black/Red: Cabernet Franc, Cabernet Sauvignon,Carmenere, Grenache, Malbec, Merlot, Nebbiolo, Pinotage, Pinot Grigio, Pinot Noir, Shiraz, Tempranillo, Zinfandel

White: Chardonnay, Gewurztraminer, Muscat Ottonel, Riesling, Sauvignon Blanc, Semillon, Viognier
